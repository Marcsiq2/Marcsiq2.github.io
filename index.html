About Me

Hello! I am Marc Siquier Penyafort, a recent graduate of the Sound and Music Computing masters program offered at the Universitat Pompeu Fabra (MTG-UPF). I am also a graduate of the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC ETSETB-TelecomBCN) holding BSc degree in Audiovisual Systems Engineering and I work as a R&D Engineer at Meridian Audio Ltd. (BSC).

My motivation for music began when I started to attend to violin classes at the age of four. After a few years I entered to the Conservatory of Mallorca where I attended violin and music classes for ten years. During those years, and probably motivated by the arrival of the first computer at home, I developed a passion for technology too. So, when I finished school I moved to Barcelona in order to take the BSc in Audiovisual Systems Engineering, studies that seemed to gather all my passions.

After graduating in 2015 I applied for the MSc in Sound and Music Computing at the UPF, linked to its Music Technology Group. Within this program I have found the link between my two main passions (music and technology) from the generation and analysis of sounds to their transmission and perception, and its analysis from a technical and computational point of view.
